What Are Crows-Feet Anti-Wrinkle Injections?

Crows-feet, also known as laugh lines or character lines, are the fine wrinkles that appear at the outer corners of the eyes. These lines develop from years of smiling, squinting, and natural facial expressions. Our crows-feet anti-wrinkle injections use botulinum toxin to soften these lines, helping you maintain a bright, youthful appearance around the eyes.

How It Works

The treatment involves precise injections of botulinum toxin into the orbicularis oculi muscle around the eye area. This temporarily relaxes the muscle, reducing the appearance of dynamic wrinkles that form when you smile or squint. The result is smoother skin around the eyes without affecting your ability to express emotions naturally.

Treatment Areas

  • Lateral canthal lines (outer eye corners)
  • Under-eye wrinkles
  • Bunny lines on the nose (can be treated simultaneously)

Treatment Duration

The procedure typically takes 15-20 minutes, making it perfect for a lunch break treatment.

Results Timeline

  • Initial effects: 3-5 days
  • Full results: 1-2 weeks
  • Duration: 3-4 months

Before & After Care

Preparation

  • Remove contact lenses if worn
  • Avoid blood-thinning medications 1 week before
  • No alcohol consumption 24 hours prior
  • Arrive with clean skin around the eyes
  • Be prepared to discuss your eye health history

Aftercare

  • Avoid rubbing or touching eyes for 24 hours
  • No eye makeup for 24 hours
  • Avoid strenuous exercise for 24 hours
  • Stay upright and avoid lying down for 4 hours
  • Skip saunas and facials for 1 week
  • Full results develop over 1-2 weeks
