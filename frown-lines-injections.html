What Are Frown-Lines Anti-Wrinkle Injections?

Frown lines, also known as glabellar lines or "11" lines, are the vertical wrinkles that appear between the eyebrows. These lines often develop from repeated frowning, concentrating, or squinting. Our frown-lines treatment uses precise botulinum toxin injections to relax the muscles responsible for these expressions, creating a smoother, more relaxed appearance in the brow area.

How It Works

The treatment targets the corrugator and procerus muscles between the eyebrows. By injecting small amounts of botulinum toxin into these specific muscles, we temporarily reduce their activity. This prevents the deep vertical lines from forming while maintaining your natural ability to express emotions. The result is a softer, more approachable appearance without the "frozen" look.

Treatment Areas

  • Vertical lines between eyebrows (glabellar region)
  • Horizontal forehead lines (can be combined)
  • Bunny lines on the nose bridge

Treatment Duration

The procedure typically takes 10-15 minutes, making it one of our quickest treatments.

Results Timeline

  • Full results: 1-2 weeks
  • Duration: 3-4 months

Before & After Care

Preparation

  • Stop blood-thinning medications 1 week prior
  • Avoid alcohol 24 hours before treatment
  • Arrive with clean, makeup-free skin
  • Be ready to discuss your brow concerns
  • No waxing or threading 1 week before

Aftercare

  • Don't massage or rub the treated area for 24 hours
  • Avoid strenuous exercise for 24 hours
  • Stay upright for 4 hours post-treatment
  • No facials or chemical peels for 1 week
  • Avoid saunas and hot tubs for 1 week
  • Results appear gradually over 1-2 weeks
