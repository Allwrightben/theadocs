What Are Lip Fillers?

Lip fillers are injectable treatments that enhance the volume, shape, and definition of the lips using hyaluronic acid-based dermal fillers. Unlike permanent fillers, hyaluronic acid fillers are temporary and can be adjusted or dissolved if needed. Our approach focuses on natural enhancement that complements your facial features and maintains lip movement and expression.

How It Works

Lip fillers work by injecting hyaluronic acid, a naturally occurring substance in the body, into the lips to add volume and definition. The filler integrates with your natural lip tissue, attracting moisture and creating plump, hydrated lips. We use advanced techniques to ensure symmetrical, natural-looking results that enhance your smile and overall facial harmony.

Treatment Areas

  • Upper and lower lip volume enhancement
  • Lip border definition
  • Cupid's bow enhancement
  • Correction of asymmetry
  • Vertical lip lines (smoker's lines)
  • Philtrum enhancement

Treatment Duration

The procedure typically takes 30-45 minutes, including consultation, numbing, and treatment time.

Results Timeline

  • Immediate volume increase
  • Full results: 1-2 weeks (as swelling subsides)
  • Duration: 6-12 months
  • Maintenance treatments every 6-9 months

Before & After Care

Preparation

  • Avoid blood-thinning medications 1 week before
  • No alcohol or aspirin 24 hours prior
  • Arrive with clean, lipstick-free lips
  • Be prepared to discuss your desired outcome
  • No dental work 2 weeks before treatment
  • Consider cold sores history (antiviral medication may be prescribed)

Aftercare

  • Apply ice packs to reduce swelling (10 minutes on/off)
  • Avoid touching or kissing lips for 24 hours
  • No strenuous exercise for 24 hours
  • Avoid hot drinks and spicy foods for 24 hours
  • No facials or chemical peels for 1 week
  • Sleep with head elevated for first 1-2 nights
  • Full results visible in 1-2 weeks
