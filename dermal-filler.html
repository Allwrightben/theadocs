What Are Dermal Fillers?

Dermal fillers are injectable treatments that restore volume and fullness to the skin, reducing the appearance of wrinkles and folds. We use hyaluronic acid-based fillers, which are naturally occurring substances in the body that attract and retain moisture. Our treatments focus on nasolabial folds (smile lines) and marionette lines to create a smoother, more youthful facial appearance.

How It Works

Dermal fillers work by replenishing lost volume in the skin. As we age, we lose collagen and hyaluronic acid, leading to sagging skin and deeper wrinkles. By injecting hyaluronic acid fillers into targeted areas, we restore volume and hydration from within. The fillers integrate seamlessly with your natural tissues, providing immediate and natural-looking results.

Treatment Areas

  • Nasolabial folds (lines from nose to mouth corners)
  • Marionette lines (lines from mouth corners downward)
  • Cheek enhancement for lift
  • Jawline definition
  • Tear troughs (under-eye hollows)

Treatment Duration

The procedure typically takes 30-45 minutes, including consultation and numbing time.

Results Timeline

  • Immediate improvement visible
  • Full results: 1-2 weeks (as swelling subsides)
  • Duration: 6-18 months depending on product and area

Before & After Care

Preparation

  • Avoid blood-thinning medications 1 week before
  • No alcohol or aspirin 24 hours prior
  • Arrive with clean, makeup-free skin
  • Be prepared to discuss your aesthetic goals
  • No dental work 2 weeks before (if treating nasolabial folds)

Aftercare

  • Apply ice packs to reduce swelling (10-15 minutes on/off)
  • Avoid touching or massaging treated areas for 24 hours
  • No strenuous exercise for 24-48 hours
  • Avoid saunas, hot tubs, and facials for 1 week
  • Sleep with head elevated for first 1-2 nights
  • Full results visible in 1-2 weeks
