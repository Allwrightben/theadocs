What Are Forehead Anti-Wrinkle Injections?

Forehead anti-wrinkle injections are a popular non-surgical cosmetic treatment that uses botulinum toxin (commonly known as Botox) to temporarily relax the muscles responsible for creating horizontal lines across the forehead. This treatment is performed by our licensed medical doctors using only FDA-approved products.

How It Works

The treatment involves carefully injecting small amounts of botulinum toxin into specific muscles in the forehead area. This temporarily blocks nerve signals to these muscles, causing them to relax and reducing the appearance of wrinkles and fine lines that form when you make facial expressions.

Treatment Areas

  • Horizontal forehead lines
  • Glabellar lines (frown lines between eyebrows)
  • Crow's feet (can be combined with forehead treatment)

Treatment Duration

The procedure typically takes 15-30 minutes, depending on the extent of treatment needed.

Results Timeline

  • Initial effects: 3-5 days
  • Full results: 1-2 weeks
  • Duration: 3-4 months

Before & After Care

Preparation

  • Avoid blood-thinning medications (aspirin, ibuprofen) 1 week before
  • No alcohol 24 hours prior to treatment
  • Arrive with clean, makeup-free skin
  • Come prepared to discuss your goals and medical history

Aftercare

  • Avoid touching or rubbing the treated area for 24 hours
  • No strenuous exercise for 24 hours
  • Stay upright for 4 hours post-treatment
  • Avoid saunas, hot tubs, and facials for 1 week
  • Results appear gradually over 1-2 weeks
