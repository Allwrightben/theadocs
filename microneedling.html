What Is Microneedling?

Microneedling, also known as collagen induction therapy, is a minimally invasive cosmetic procedure that uses fine needles to create tiny punctures in the skin. This controlled skin injury stimulates the body's natural healing process, triggering collagen and elastin production. The result is improved skin texture, reduced appearance of scars, fine lines, and pigmentation issues.

How It Works

The treatment uses a specialized device with sterile, fine needles that create microscopic channels in the skin. These micro-injuries stimulate the body's wound healing response, increasing blood flow and triggering the production of collagen and elastin. Growth factors are released, and new skin cells are generated, leading to firmer, smoother, and more youthful-looking skin.

Treatment Areas

  • Face (full face or targeted areas)
  • Neck and décolletage
  • Acne scars and surgical scars
  • Fine lines and wrinkles
  • Skin texture irregularities
  • Hyperpigmentation and sun damage
  • Stretch marks

Treatment Duration

The procedure typically takes 45-60 minutes, including consultation, numbing time, and treatment.

Results Timeline

  • Initial improvement: 1-2 weeks
  • Progressive results: 4-6 weeks
  • Full results: 3-6 months
  • Duration: Long-lasting with maintenance

Number of Sessions

Most patients see optimal results with 3-6 treatments spaced 4-6 weeks apart, followed by maintenance sessions every 6-12 months.

Before & After Care

Preparation

  • Avoid retinoids, exfoliants, and harsh skincare 1 week before
  • No waxing, chemical peels, or laser treatments 2 weeks prior
  • Arrive with clean, makeup-free skin
  • Inform us of any medical conditions or medications
  • Avoid sun exposure and tanning 2 weeks before
  • Consider herpes history (antiviral medication may be prescribed)

Aftercare

  • Apply prescribed soothing products immediately after
  • Use gentle, fragrance-free skincare for 5-7 days
  • Avoid sun exposure and use SPF 30+ daily
  • No makeup for 24 hours
  • Avoid saunas, hot tubs, and strenuous exercise for 1 week
  • No retinoids or exfoliants for 1 week
  • Skin may appear pink/red for 24-48 hours
