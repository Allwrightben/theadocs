About Us

Doctors Lia Hart and Nikita Tank met while working at the Watford hospital and quickly realised they shared a dream to provide beauty treatments from trained medical hands, and together they started The Aesthetics Docs to live this dream.

Both are fully qualified doctors with the NHS, and have also undergone expert training at The Derma Institute in London - a globally recognised leader in aesthetics medical education.

We believe in natural-looking results that enhance your features not change them.
